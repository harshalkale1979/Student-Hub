How it all connects

1. Track the numbers

Your GPA and attendance live on this dashboard the moment you save them in their tools — no copying between pages.

2. Plan around your timetable

The Study Planner and Timetable Generator share the same subject list, so your tasks line up with real class slots.

3. Revise with notes & videos

Tag notes by subject, then jump to matching video lessons in the library when a topic needs more explanation.
